CIA

CIA stands for Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability whic are also known as CIA Triad.

This a model design that are used for cybersecurity and are used to create security policies.

Confidentiality

This ensures that unauthorize receivers cannot understand the indormation/data.

Integrity

Ensures that the information/data cannot be tampared or changed during the transaction. This make sure the data is accurate and relaiable.

Availability

This verify the sender is sending the right receiver is the correct party.
